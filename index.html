About Us

Athenian Riviera Cruises specializes in day and evening fun cruises with a duration of 5-6 hours, our Blue Summer Cruises are the perfect way to spend a day or evening out on and in the water.

One of the highlights of our Blue Summer Cruises is the opportunity to swim in the dazzling crystal waters of Aegina Moni and Hydroussa. These locations boast some of the most beautiful beaches in Greece, and our guests will have the chance to explore them at their leisure.

So why wait? Book your Blue Summer Cruise with Athenian Riviera Cruises today and discover the beauty of Greece's coastline like never before.

Cruises Offerings

Blue Day Cruise

Our Blue Summer day cruises feature music and relaxation on board, as well as water fun and water toys for those who want to make a splash. And for those who want to take things up a notch, we offer optional choices including lunch on board, and a variety of beverage and alcohol drinks.

We also provide transfer pick up from your hotel and back, making it easy for you to join us on our Blue Summer Cruises.

Whether you're a solo traveler, a couple, or a group of friends or family, Athenian Riviera Cruises has something for everyone. Our friendly and experienced crew is here to ensure that you have the best possible experience on our day cruises.

Fantastic Night Cruise

Our evening cruises are an unforgettable way to experience the beauty of the Greek riviera after dark. Take a swim in the amazing blue waters of Hydroussa, a picturesque island in the Saronic Gulf. Guests will be able to enjoy a refreshing swim in the warm evening breeze, a truly unique experience.

But the highlight is undoubtedly the sunset experience. Guests will have the chance to witness one of the most stunning sunsets in Greece, as the sun dips below the horizon in a blaze of orange and pink.

As the evening progresses, our guests will be treated to live music, adding to the already unforgettable atmosphere. Dance the night away under the stars, with the sound of the sea as your backdrop.
